Created a data framework in R to automate analysis of meteorlogic data collected on Mount Kaʻala, Oʻahu. Co-authored poster presented at the American Geophysical Union Fall 2020 (Shown above).

Using the R programming language I automated the analysis of meteorlogic data collected on Mount Kaʻala Oʻahu’s highest Mountain. The results from this analysis were presented at the American Geophysical Unions fall meeting.
